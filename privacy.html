Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 9, 2026

1. Overview

MonitorContas ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we access, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

2. Data We Access via TikTok API

When you connect your TikTok account, the App requests access to the following data through TikTok's official API:

  • user.info.profile — Your display name, username, and avatar image.
  • user.info.stats — Your follower count, following count, and total likes.
  • video.list — Your published videos and their performance metrics (views, likes, comments).

3. How We Use Your Data

All data accessed from TikTok is used solely to display your own account information back to you within the App's dashboard. We do not:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share your data with any external services
  • Store your TikTok data on external servers
  • Use your data for advertising or profiling

4. Data Storage

The App processes your TikTok data locally in your browser session. No personal data is stored on external servers or databases beyond what TikTok itself retains.

5. Authentication & Tokens

Access to your TikTok account is handled via OAuth 2.0. Your TikTok password is never seen or stored by the App. You may revoke the App's access at any time by visiting your TikTok account settings under "Manage app permissions".

6. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the TikTok API. Your use of TikTok is governed by TikTok's own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service. We are not responsible for TikTok's data practices.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the updated effective date at the top of this page.

9. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the App's GitHub repository.
